1Quick Start — the core loop

GREEN LIFE is a grow game where your plants earn $GREEN for you over time. The basic loop:

  1. Start a farm — a one-time 0.25 SOL buy-in mints your Level 1 farm and hands you a welcome kit (2 plants + 1 boost + a guaranteed Weedster Egg that cracks on the spot).
  2. Plant strains into your grow slots. Each strain has a Grow Power (GP) — that's your share of the rewards.
  3. Water them so they don't wilt, and wait out the grow cycle.
  4. Harvest & claim your $GREEN.
  5. Reinvest — buy packs, breed rarer strains, upgrade your farm, level your grower, or trade on the market. Repeat, bigger.
🌿 The whole economy runs on one idea: 420 billion $GREEN, minted once, never again. Rewards flow from a fixed pool that drains slowly over years, and everything you spend in-game is burned forever. No infinite printing.

5The Grow Cycle, Watering & Death

This is the most important section — read it so nothing surprises you.

Grow cycle length

  • Normal plant: 30 days to a full harvest.
  • Mythical: 15 days · Fast Auto: a flat 7 days.
  • Your grower grow/harvest bonus + kitchen buffs can shorten Normal & Mythical cycles (never below 30% of the base). Fast Auto's 7 days is fixed and never changes.

Watering

  • You can water a plant every 8 hours.
  • There's a 24-hour grace window — miss watering and nothing bad happens for the first day.
  • After the grace, a dry plant loses 10% of its Grow Power per day overdue (up to 100%). Water it and full power is restored.
  • 7 full days with zero water = the plant dies. Mythicals are the exception — they never need water. Fast Autos do need water like normal plants.

Pulling a Normal plant early — the day windows

⚠️ Before Day 22, a Normal plant DIES if you remove or harvest it — the card and its harvest pot are gone. This is the single biggest way players lose plants. Let them mature.
What you get when you pull a Normal plant, by age

Don't sit on a finished plant

Once a plant is ready, you have a 7-day grace to claim it. Ignore it longer and it counts as neglect: a Normal plant dies and its pot is burned. (A finished plant shows a 🌾 CLAIM countdown so you know the deadline without opening it.)

ℹ️ Pulling early only ever risks that plant's harvest pot and its survival — it never touches the $GREEN you've already been streaming to your claimable balance (see §6).

6Earning $GREEN — emissions & the 70/30 split

Rewards come out of a fixed emissions pool (half of the 420B supply). Each day the pool releases 0.025% of whatever remains — a slow geometric drain with about a 7.6-year half-life. It never re-mints.

Your cut of the day's emissions is your share of total Grow Power: the more GP you're growing versus the whole network, the more $GREEN you earn every second.

The 70 / 30 split

Every plant's fair earnings are split two ways, always adding up to 100% — never more:

  • 70% streams continuously into your claimable balance — claim it anytime.
  • 30% is locked into that plant's harvest pot, which pays out when the grow cycle finishes (or partially/penalized on an early pull, per §5).
🔥 Burn on loss. If you forfeit a harvest pot — pulling too early, letting a plant die, or neglecting a finished one — that $GREEN is burned, not returned to the pool. Spent supply is gone forever.

7Fast Autos — the speed lane

Fast Autos are a second grow area for a quick, high-intensity payout.

  • Same total, faster: a Fast Auto pays ×30/7 (~4.29×) the Grow Power over a flat 7-day cycle — so a 7-day Auto earns roughly the same total $GREEN as that strain's full 30-day Normal harvest, just four-plus times faster.
  • Grow once, then it's gone. Harvesting a Fast Auto consumes the plant — it doesn't replant.
  • Water them like normal plants (same grace/penalty/death rules) — they are not drought-proof.
  • No boosts. Plant boost items do nothing on Autos, and nothing shortens the 7 days.
  • Never pull early. Pulling a Fast Auto before Day 7 kills it — 100% loss, pot burned. There's no partial early harvest.
  • Spots: 2 / 4 / 6 / 8 / 10 by farm level, unlocked in pairs (see §2).

8Mythicals — the top tier

Mythicals are one-of-a-kind cards you can only get by breeding or cracking a Weedster Egg — never from packs. They grow in the Mythical Tent.

  • Grow Power range: ~100 up to a hard cap of 1,000,000. (A low-tier breeding jackpot can make a small Mythical; you climb its GP by breeding.) Egg-born Mythicals roll 150,000–600,000.
  • Cycle: 15 days. Drought-proof: they never need water. Boost-immune: plant boost items don't affect them (their power comes from breeding).
  • They never die on harvest. Instead, a harvested Mythical is re-minted as a fresh card — same name, art, and lineage — at −10% Grow Power. You re-breed to climb back up. This keeps a maxed Mythical from ever being "finished."
  • Ready harvest: pays the full pot and re-mints at −10%. Early pull: the pot is forfeited (burned), but the card survives and still re-mints at −10%.

11Breeding — chasing rarer genetics

Breeding fuses two cards into new ones. It's instant, capped at 25 breeds/day, and both parents are destroyed on success (the one exception: a Mythical parent survives a failed Mythical×Normal attempt). All breed costs are USD-pegged, paid in $GREEN.

Normal × Normal

Cost = sum of both parents' card cost: Common $0.15 · Uncommon $0.30 · Rare $0.60 · Epic $1.25 · Legendary $2.50.

Success: 50% base (up to 95% with grower/avatar bonuses). Two things can happen on a success:

  • Jackpot → a Mythical. A small chance fuses the pair into a one-of-a-kind Cross Breed Mythical whose GP = the sum of both parents' GP. Jackpot odds by tier: Common 2.5% · Uncommon 5% · Rare 7.5% · Epic 10% · Legendary 15% (mixed pairs use the average). Higher-GP parents → a stronger Mythical.
  • Otherwise → offspring cards. You get 1 / 2 / 3 cards (50% / 35% / 15%), rarity weighted toward the lower parent (Mythical is unreachable this way — that's the jackpot's job).

A failed Normal breed still salvages 3 consolation boosts, so it's never a total waste.

Mythical + Normal

Cost = the Mythical's GP-bracket price + the normal card's cost (normal card here: 1★ $0.10 · 2★ $0.25 · 3★ $0.50 · 4★ $1.25 · 5★ $2.50).

15PvP — Rob / Raid

Send your crew to raid rival farms for their $GREEN.

  • Up to 20 raids/day.
  • Raid power = your combined boosted Grow Power (planted + Mythicals + Fast Autos), scaled by your grower multiplier.
  • Win chance = your power ÷ (your power + their power), always between 10% and 90% — never a sure thing either way.
  • Fee: 20% of the win amount, charged up front and burned on every attempt — win or lose. A loss costs you only that fee.
  • Anti-grief shield: rob someone successfully and they're shielded for 3 hours. Attacking anyone drops your own shield, so raiders are exposed too.
ℹ️ PvP is a simulated v1 today; real on-chain player-vs-player loot transfer is on the roadmap.

16PvP — Spin The Bong

A group betting game: 2–10 players ante an equal $GREEN bet into a pot, the bong spins, and it pays out.

  • Pot fee: 5% — split 2.5% burned + 2.5% to the buyback-and-burn treasury. The winner takes the other 95%.
  • Three modes:
    • 🎯 Heads Up — 1v1, 50/50, one spin decides it.
    • 🏆 Winner Take All — one spin lands on a single winner.
    • 💀 Elimination — each spin knocks one player out until one remains; the eliminated stay to spectate.

20Playstyles & game loops

There's no single "right" way to play — GREEN LIFE supports several loops, and most growers blend a few. Here are the main ones:

🌿 The Steady Grower Low risk · F2P-friendly

Buy in, plant Normals, water daily, harvest on Day 30, claim, and reinvest the $GREEN into more plants and cheap packs. Level your grower and farm as you go. The safest, most passive path — great if you just want to log in, water, and stack.

⚡ The Speed Farmer Higher tempo

Lean on Fast Autos for full-size payouts every 7 days and reinvest fast to compound. You trade convenience for speed — you must water on time and never pull early, or it's a total loss. Best once you can afford several auto spots.

🧬 The Breeder Long game · high ceiling

Collect strong strains and breed for jackpot Mythicals (or crack eggs), grow them in the tent, then re-breed to climb their Grow Power toward the 1M cap. The path to the biggest earners and the rarest, most valuable cards.

💹 The Trader Market-focused

Work the marketplace — buy under-priced cards, combine Commons up the ladder, flip Legendaries and eggs, and time the floors. You can play almost entirely through the market if you read value well.

🍳 The Chef Farm Lv3+

Run the Kitchen: cook grower boosts to supercharge your whole farm during big harvest windows, or sell the premium ones (they're scarce, so they hold value) to other growers.

🎮 The Raider Aggressive · risk

Build combined power and run up to 20 raids a day for other growers' $GREEN. Manage your shield, pick your targets, and accept the burn on every attempt. High action, high variance.

🎰 The Gambler High variance

Live in Spin The Bong — ante up and go for pots across the three modes. Pure risk/reward, on your terms.

🤝 The Connector Social growth

Post daily and grow a referral network for a passive multi-level cut of everything your downline does — plus a steady drip of egg ticks toward free Mythical shots. The more people you bring, the more you earn without touching a plant.
