What is GREEN LIFE

A play-to-earn grow game built to last.

GREEN LIFE is a cannabis farming game on the Solana blockchain. You start a grow, plant strains, water and tend them, then harvest for $GREEN — the game's token. Trade cards on a player marketplace, breed rare Mythical strains, open packs, and take on other growers in PvP. It plays like a game first — the earning is baked into the growing.

Tokenomics

Fixed supply. Real scarcity.

Most emission games print tokens forever until the reward is worthless. GREEN LIFE does the opposite: the entire 420,000,000,000 $GREEN supply is minted one time at launch and the contract can never mint again. Rewards come out of a single shared emissions pool that drains slowly over years — and everything players spend in-game is burned, permanently shrinking the supply.

🔒

Minted once

420B $GREEN created at launch. The mint authority is burned — no team faucet, no surprise inflation, ever.

🔥

Deflationary by design

Breeding, packs, upgrades, marketplace fees — every $GREEN spent in-game is burned forever, not recycled.

🌊

Slow, fair emissions

Rewards flow from one pool that decays ~0.025%/day (about a 7.6-year half-life), so early growers don't drain it and there's runway for years.

How to play

Plant → Grow → Harvest → Earn.

Your farm's total Grow Power is your share of the emissions pool — the more power you grow, the more $GREEN you farm every second. Choose how you want to grow:

Steady🌿

Normal Plants

A 30-day grow cycle. The only plant you can supercharge with boost items. The steady, long-game backbone of your farm.

Fast⚡

Fast Autos

A flat 7-day grow-once. Earns a full month's payout in a week, but can't be boosted and pulls a one-and-done harvest. High speed, high commitment.

Rare🧬

Mythicals

Bred from two strains or cracked from a Weedster Egg — never found in packs. One-of-a-kind power that you climb through breeding.

Plants need water or they wilt. Pull too early and you forfeit the grow. Let one finish and you claim the full payout — then reinvest into more plants, upgrades, and rarer genetics.

Built to be fair

Provably fair, on-chain.

🎲

Verifiable randomness (VRF)

Every random outcome — packs, breeds, PvP spins — resolves on-chain through a Verifiable Random Function. No one, not even the house, can rig a result.

🔗

On-chain ownership

Your farm, cards, and $GREEN live on Solana — yours to hold, trade, or sell. Temporary accounts refund their rent back to you on settle.

🔥

Burn-on-spend

Forfeited and spent $GREEN is burned, not pocketed. The economy tightens as the game grows.

🔒

No infinite mint

420B, one time. The math is fixed at launch and can't be changed to dilute holders later.

FAQ

Straight answers.

Is GREEN LIFE live? Can I buy the token?

No. The game is in active development, targeting launch in roughly 3–6 months. No $GREEN token exists yet, nothing is for sale, and anyone claiming to sell $GREEN today is not us.

What makes it "fixed-supply"?

The full 420 billion $GREEN is minted a single time at launch and the mint authority is burned. No inflation, no team faucet, no way to print more later.

How do I actually earn?

You grow plants. Your total Grow Power is your share of a shared emissions pool, paying out $GREEN over time. You can also trade cards, breed rare Mythicals, and win PvP.

What stops it from crashing like other P2E games?

No infinite minting, a pool that emits slowly over years, and a burn on everything spent in-game — so supply tightens instead of ballooning as more people play.

Which blockchain?

Solana — fast and cheap, so growing, trading, and PvP feel like a game, not a gas bill.

Is it fair / can outcomes be rigged?

Random outcomes resolve on-chain via a Verifiable Random Function (VRF). No one — player, team, or validator — can predict or fix a result.
